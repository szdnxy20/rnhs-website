STRAND WE OFFER

We are thrilled to introduce you to the exciting opportunities and unique educational pathway offered within our program. Our strand is specifically designed to provide students with a focused and specialized learning experience, tailored to their interests and future career goals.


Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand.

TVL Strand is designed to develop students' skills that is useful for livelihood and technical projects. It provides a curriculum that is a combination of Core Courses and specialized hands-on courses that meets the competency-based assessment of TESDA.



Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand. This strand is designed for students who have a passion for the social sciences, arts, and humanities.



Accountancy, Business and Management The Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) strand would focus on the basic concepts of financial management, business management, corporate operations, and all things that are accounted for.



Science, technology, engineering and mathematics. (STEM) is an approach to learning and development that integrates the areas of science, technology, engineering and mathematics. Through STEM, students develop key skills including: problem solving. creativity. critical analysis.

For students interested in business and entrepreneurship, we offer the General Academic Strand (GAS). This strand provides a broad-based curriculum that covers various academic disciplines, including mathematics, sciences, languages, and social sciences.
